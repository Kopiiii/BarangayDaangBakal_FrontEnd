Certificate of Residency

Description:
A Certificate of Residency is a document issued by the local government unit (LGU) of the Philippines certifying that a person is a resident of a particular barangay, municipality, or city within its jurisdiction. This document may be required for various purposes such as availing of government services, applying for a job, enrolling in school, or applying for a business permit.

Requirements:

1. Valid ID
2. Proof of Residency

Barangay Clearance

Description:
A Barangay Clearance is a document issued by the barangay (the smallest administrative division in the Philippines) certifying that a person is a resident of the barangay and has no pending legal or administrative obligations within the community. This document may be required for various purposes such as applying for a job, traveling abroad, applying for a business permit, or availing of government services.

Requirements:

Certificate of Being Indigent

Description:
A Certificate of Indigency is a document issued by the local government unit (LGU) in the Philippines certifying that a person is indigent and therefore eligible for certain social services and programs provided by the government. This certificate is usually required for availing free or discounted healthcare services, educational assistance, and other welfare benefits.

Description:
